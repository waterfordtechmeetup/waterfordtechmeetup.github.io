Our Next Event

Wednesday, January 25th, 2017 at 7PM
BoxWorks coworking space | 40 Patrick St, Waterford
RSVP at meetup.com

Ladies and gentlemen, the Waterford Tech Meetup is back! On Wednesday the 25th January we are inviting all interested to join us for a talk on how to update your website on GitHub Pages through Dropbox, followed by a talk on the value of constraints within software engineering.

The meetup starts at 7:00pm, with food and drinks provided.

Afterwards we are looking forward to discussions and socialising.

About

The Waterford Tech Meetup is a chance to socialise with (and learn from) like-minded people to discuss wide ranging topics of a technical nature. We meet on the last Wednesday of every month with the aim to have 1 or 2 talks, with ample time for socialising.

We hope to see you at the next meetup!
